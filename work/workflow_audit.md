# Workflow Audit

## Recurring Tasks

| Task | Classification | Why |
|------|----------------|-----|
| Solving DSA problems on LeetCode or Codeforces | Just me | I need to attempt problems myself to build real problem-solving ability. |
| Debugging code after I make an attempt | Collaborate with AI | AI can help identify mistakes, but I should understand and fix the issue myself. |
| Learning Spring Boot, SQL, ML, and backend concepts | Collaborate with AI | AI helps explain difficult concepts, while I verify them through practice. |
| Completing FlyRank internship assignments | Collaborate with AI | AI can help structure the work, but I must run the analysis and verify every result. |
| Writing internship reports and documentation | Delegate to AI with review | AI can draft and organize the writing, but I must check technical accuracy. |
| Analyzing datasets and interpreting results | Collaborate with AI | AI can suggest analysis ideas, while I execute the code and judge the findings. |
| Preparing technical interview answers | Collaborate with AI | AI can simulate interview questions, but I need to practise answering independently. |
| Customizing resumes and cover letters | Delegate to AI with review | AI can tailor the wording, but all claims must match my actual experience. |
| Searching for internships and campus opportunities | Delegate to AI with review | AI can summarize roles and eligibility, but I make the final application decision. |
| Planning my weekly study schedule | Delegate to AI with review | AI can suggest a realistic plan, but I adjust it based on deadlines and energy. |
| Writing boilerplate code and standard templates | Fully automate | Repetitive templates can be generated automatically and checked quickly. |
| Converting long notes into revision summaries | Fully automate | AI can compress notes efficiently without changing the core content. |
| Choosing which job offer or company to join | Just me | The final decision depends on my goals, risk tolerance, location, and personal priorities. |

## Three Target Tasks

### 1. Debugging DSA Solutions

**Success definition**
- I understand why my solution is wrong.
- I can fix it myself after the explanation.
- The explanation includes time and space complexity.

---

### 2. Writing Internship Reports

**Success definition**
- The writing is technically accurate.
- No results or numbers are invented.
- It needs only minor edits before submission.

---

### 3. Technical Interview Preparation

**Success definition**
- Questions match software engineering interviews.
- Answers are concise and technically correct.
- I can confidently explain the concepts without reading notes.
